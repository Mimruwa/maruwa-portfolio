About

Bridging science, healthcare, innovation, and personality

Maruwa is a pharmacy graduate from North South University, Bangladesh, and a current Master's student in Translational Medicine at the University of Helsinki, Finland. Her academic and practical background spans pharmaceutical sciences, clinical and community pharmacy exposure, industrial pharmaceutical practice, research methodology, computer-aided drug discovery, precision medicine, public health, and healthcare innovation.

Her long-term goal is to contribute to drug discovery, pharmaceutical research, translational bioinformatics, diagnostics, and equitable healthcare improvement through rigorous science, interdisciplinary collaboration, and patient-centered thinking. She also loves bringing warmth, creativity, and a playful cat-inspired visual identity to her personal brand.
